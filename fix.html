.Fix

Autofixer solutions

Dotfix is an artificial intelligence chat box created by Autofixer Solutions which will enable our customers obtain expert advice answer technical questions and give customers updates about the sate of automobiles. In addition, Dotfix or as we like to call it “your virtual Mechanic” can help identify faults in your cars give you first aid to stop the fault from becoming dangerous to your automobile and book a fix for you at Autofixer solutions where the problem can be completely solved. Because its virtual, it can be accessed from the convenient of your home, while driving and at your office. Dotfix is aimed to give car owners first hand knowledge about the potential problem and recommend a solution both long term and short term. Dotfix can be accessed via various social media platform such as Facebook, Instagram and WhatsApp.
